What services do I provide

Photography

I work with a Canon M50, using both a 15–45mm and a 70–200mm lens to capture a wide range of perspectives, from detailed close-ups to distant subjects.

My focus is on clean composition, natural light, and creating images that feel authentic and visually strong.

Web Design

I design and build modern, responsive websites using HTML and CSS. My approach combines clean structure, thoughtful layout, and user-friendly design to create websites that look good, perform well, and work smoothly across all devices.

Motion Design

In motion design, I create animations and visual content using Adobe After Effects. I focus on smooth motion, clear storytelling, and visuals that enhance digital experiences, whether for websites, social media, or video content.
